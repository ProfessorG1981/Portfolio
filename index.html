Who Could Imagine A King

On this project I used the digital audio workstation (DAW) Logic Pro to pre-record the piano audio track. After creating the instumental track I video recorded myself playing the same song and sent the track to the soloist who then video recorded utilizing earbuds to sing and record with the track. My approach was to only capture her vocal recording without the track. I then used a video editor to combine the two videos and used Logic Pro to combined her audio track with the pre-recorded instrumental. Lastly, I aligned and synchronized the tracks with both vidoes and mastered the final product.

I Open My Mouth

I rearranged this spitual selection and pre-recorded all vocal parts and overdubs usuing (DAW) Logic Pro. Finally, I aligned and synchronized the tracks with the video then mixed and mastered the final product.

Snow Falls

Technology integration has allowed me to be more creative when creating student performances. I created a virtual choir performance using the digital audio workstation (DAW) Logic Pro and video editor to create the virtual choir performance. I asked students to video record themselves singing the song "Snow Falls", using the instrumental track playing in their ear with the goal of recieving their vocals without the instrumental. I edited and aligned the new student recordings with a pre-recorded version of the chorus singing this selection and added overdubs for a clean choral mix.
